NGREDIENTS:
2 cucumbers
8 celery stalks
4 apples
16 leaves kale with stalks
1 lemon
2 in (5 cm) piece of ginger

DIRECTIONS:
Wash all produce well.
Peel the lemon and core apple.
Add all ingredients through juicer and enjoy!
Servings: Makes 2 servings of 16-20 oz (500-600 ml)

Nutrition per serving:
Calories125
Fat0.5 g
Sodium64 mg
Carbohydrates27 g
Fiber1 g
Sugars15 g
Protein 3 g
